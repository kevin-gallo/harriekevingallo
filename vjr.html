Your Junk Removal Questions Answered

Below are some common questions customers ask and our answers.
Call or contact us if you have other questions or concerns, and we’ll happily answer them.

How much do you charge for mattress removal?

We base our rates on volume (how much space your mattress takes up) and include labor, transportation, and disposal in our free estimates and upfront quotes.

What kinds of cleanouts can you do?

We offer hands-off and stress-free cleanouts to homeowners, contractors, businesses, and property managers in Montgomery County, PA and surrounding counties. Our cleanout services include

  • apartment cleanouts
  • garage & attic cleanouts
  • shed cleanouts
  • house cleanouts
  • estate cleanouts
  • office cleanouts
  • retail cleanouts
  • warehouse cleanouts
  • storage unit cleanouts
  • eviction & foreclosure cleanouts
  • business clear-outs
  • and more!

We work around your schedule to clear out your spaces quickly and safely.

Can you remove pools?

We can remove above-ground and in-ground pools. We tidy the debris and ensure the space is ready for whatever comes next. Talk to us about your old pool today!

Do you provide construction debris cleanup services?

We work with Montgomery area homeowners, businesses, contractors, and house flippers to keep their worksites safe and free of debris during and after construction or renovation.

You can depend on Venture Junk Removal for fast, affordable construction debris removal and site cleanup services.

What do you do with my junk?

We do what we can to keep your junk out of the landfills. Our team donates gently used household and business items to local charities, like Goodwill, and recycles everything possible. You can count on us for eco-friendly junk removal solutions that give back.

Do I have to be there when you take my junk?

We offer curbside and contactless services for your convenience. As a licensed and insured junk removal company, we can take care of most of your decluttering needs without you being there while we do (although watching that junk disappear is quite satisfying). Talk to us to learn more.

Do you handle hoarding cleanups?

No junk job is too small, complex, or messy for us! We work with Montgomery area realtors, property managers, and organizers to remove hoarder junk quickly and safely.

Book a hoarding cleanup today!

Can you help with urgent apartment trashouts?

With dedicated junk removal professionals ready anytime, we can handle urgent junk removal needs, including apartment trashouts. We work with landlords and property managers throughout Montgomery County, PA and surrounding counties to help them get apartments and other properties rent-ready fast.

Call now for fast, affordable trashout services!
